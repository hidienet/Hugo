![](/images/diptyquepopup5.jpg)

![](/images/diptyquepopup6.jpg)

![](/images/diptyquepopup7.jpg)

![](/images/diptyquepopup8.jpg)

試聞吓隻“剃鬚膏”味演變嘅過程
